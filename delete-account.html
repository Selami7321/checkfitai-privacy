Delete Your Account

Users can permanently delete their CheckFit AI account directly inside the application.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the CheckFit AI app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

When an account is deleted:

  • User profile data is removed
  • Associated personal data is deleted
  • Access to the account is permanently revoked
